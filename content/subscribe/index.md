You can also subscribe to a tag by appending `/index.xml`  to the end of the URL. For example, to follow the `haiku` tag at 
{{< highlight shell >}}
https://pranshugaba.com/tags/haiku
{{< /highlight >}}
go to
{{< highlight shell >}}
https://pranshugaba.com/tags/haiku/index.xml
{{< /highlight >}}
