There lay a shopping trolley in the hall,  
so far away from the shopping mall.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It was utterly lost,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;its path uncrossed  
with no one there to look at it at all.
