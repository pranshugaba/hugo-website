There once was a cat named Toad,  
Who loved to take naps on the road.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He was very displeased,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;When woken from sleep,  
Yet he decided to let it go.
