Two red ants called Mary and Jake,  
one fine day, they went to the lake.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mary had some coffee,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and Jake ate a toffee,  
but none of them found any cake.
