Cycling home from TIFR

An early morning coastal ride

I cycled home from TIFR. I left at 5.45 am and reached home by 9.15 am.

In spite of taking a longer route today, I was surprisingly much less tired compared to the last time I cycled to TIFR.

I got to see Rainbow on the way!
