Sunday, 3 May 2020

liquefy

a poem

a late afternoon —
     chocolate and vanilla
        cool delicious chocobar

but lo and behold —
     misfortune befalls
        the ice-cream falls

liquefied dessert —
     will now be eaten
        by a simian
