About me

Hi, I am Pranshu!

I am a PhD student in Theoretical Computer Science at TIFR, Mumbai. Previously, I did my undergraduate studies in Maths at IISc, Bangalore.

My research interests include Automata Theory, Logic, Discrete Maths, and Algorithms.

I write poems and puzzles, and develop apps and games. I also love photography, swimming and cycling but not both at once.
