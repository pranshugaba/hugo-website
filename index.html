Hello there!

Welcome to my site

I am a PhD research scholar (nearing completion) in Theoretical Computer Science at TIFR Mumbai. I am currently looking for postdoc opportunities!

My primary area of research during my PhD has been reactive synthesis for probabilistic systems, i.e., finding efficient algorithms to synthesize winning strategies (of small memory size) for conjunctions of probabilistic objectives in stochastic games on graphs. You can read more about my work in my CV.

On this site, you will also find my blog where I write and share interesting articles, puzzles, poems, photos, recipes, comics, and so much more.
